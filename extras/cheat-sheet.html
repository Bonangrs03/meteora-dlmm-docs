1. Core Formulas

Constant Product (AMM)
x · y = k
x = token A in pool, y = token B in pool, k = constant. Buying drains A → price rises. Selling adds A → price falls. Ch 5
AMM Price
P = y / x
Price of token A in terms of token B. When you buy A, x ↓ so P ↑. When you sell A, x ↑ so P ↓. Ch 5
DLMM Dynamic Fee
Fee = base + variable
Base fee: floor set at pool creation. Variable fee: rises with volatility and bin-crossing. Capped at 10% max. Ch 10
Impermanent Loss
IL = 1 − HODL_value / LP_value
Compare what you'd have by holding vs what your LP position is worth. Positive IL = LPing underperformed. Ch 7
True LP Profit
Profit = (Withdrawal Value + Fees Claimed) − Gross Deposit
Always compare to HODL Profit = HODL Value − Gross Deposit. If LP Profit > HODL Profit → you won. Ch 14

5. Wallet Setup Checklist

  1. Install Phantom/Solflare/Backpack wallet (phantom.app)
  2. Write seed phrase on PAPER — never screenshot, never digital
  3. Set strong password — 12+ chars, unique
  4. Buy SOL from exchange (Binance, Coinbase, etc.)
  5. Withdraw SOL to wallet address (check "Receive" in Phantom)
  6. Swap some SOL for USDC via Jupiter (jup.ag) inside wallet
  7. Keep ~0.01 SOL for gas (~$2 at $200/SOL — covers 5,000+ txs)
  8. Go to app.meteora.ag → Connect Wallet → Phantom
  9. Search SOL-USDC pool with 20–50 bps bin step
  10. Create position: Spot strategy, ±10% range, $20–50 first deposit
  11. Bookmark position URL — take screenshot of pool address
  12. Note entry SOL price — your reference for measuring IL later
✅ MIN START: $20–50
⛽ TX COST: ~$0.0002
🕐 TIME: ~5 min

6. Risk Management Rules

#1
Check Total Fees First
Never LP a pool with <25 SOL total fees generated. Low fees = low genuine volume or fake activity.
#2
Vet the Token
Check Rugcheck.xyz + Bubblemaps. If 3 wallets hold 90% supply → RUN.
#3
Start Small
First position = money you can lose entirely. Treat it as tuition. Learn the mechanics before scaling.
#4
Don't Chase Extreme APR
1000% APR = usually temporary hype, hidden risk, or IL waiting to wreck you.
🚨 When to Exit Immediately
Trigger Action
Token rugging (sudden 90%+ drop) CLOSE NOW
Pool abandoned (no trades for days) WITHDRAW
Fee APR <5% for >1 month CONSIDER EXIT
IL growing for weeks, no reversal REVIEW
Volume declining month over month MONITOR
🩺 Monthly LP Health Check
① Tracking PnL honestly? (HODL comparison)   ② Strategy still right for market?   ③ Checking at right frequency?   ④ Fees worth the time spent?   ⑤ Do you understand WHY you performed that way?
⚡ GOLDEN RULE: LPing is NOT free money. You are selling volatility and getting paid in fees.
